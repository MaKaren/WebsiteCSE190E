Cleveland STEM High School Tennis
(2016 - 2020)

I represented Cleveland’s tennis team and played for Junior Varsity in my early years of high school and Varsity later on.

I did something called Tennis Quick Start from 2017 to 2018 during every summer from June to August, 2 to 5 days a week. I helped coach childrens from ages 5 to 11 to play tennis.

I was also involved in Cleveland's Tennis Club in 2017 to 2018. We would play tennis during the first few weeks of school, September to February for 5 days a week. In this club I would also help coaching middle schoolers and high schoolers for readiness before the tennis season.

Awards/Honors

  • 10th Grade (2018) Tennis JV/Varsity - The most influence in Junior varsity performance.
    Awarded represents I was the one to help influence and bring the most out of all the Junior varsity members.
  • 11th Grade (2019) Tennis Varsity - Rookie of the year.
